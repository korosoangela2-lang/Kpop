About EXO

EXO debuted in 2012 under SM Entertainment and is known for strong vocals and choreography.

EXO consists of members Suho, Xiumin, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai, and Sehun. They are known for their powerful vocals, intricate choreography, and diverse music styles. EXO has achieved global success with hits like "Growl," "Call Me Baby," and "Love Shot." They have also won numerous awards and have a dedicated fanbase known as EXO-Ls. With their continued success and influence in the K-pop industry, EXO remains one of the most popular and influential boy bands in the world.
